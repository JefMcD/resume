Comic Wiki

Github: Comix Wiki

Summary :

A Wiki App for comic fans that allows users to Search, Create and Edit encyclopedia entries. Entries are created in Markdown then converted to HTML and then stored in the app and searcheable. Project makes extensive use of Regex expressions for converting markdown into html and for performing searches.

Tech: Django:Python:html:css
